# **AIforCoffee**-
I am developing an innovation project titled ‘AI-Driven Market Intelligence for Ethiopian Coffee Using Social Media Sentiment Analysis.’ The goal is to analyze global Twitter (X) data using Natural Language Processing (NLP) and transformer-based models to extract customer sentiment, preferences, and trends about Ethiopian coffee

## **Global Coffee Consumer Insights** 
### **Key Consumer Trends & Preferences** :

**Brewing/Preparation Methods:** Strong shift to at-home specialty brewing (pour-over, French press, espresso machines at home) alongside café culture. Cold brew and nitro cold brew dominate for refreshment — smoother, lower acidity, easy to make in batches. RTD (ready-to-drink) coffee exploding (projected 6–7%+ CAGR), especially canned nitro or concentrates that need only water/milk. Gen Z prefers cold options year-round.
Flavor Expectations:
**Loves:** Bright acidity, fruity/tropical notes (passion fruit, mango, citrus, berry), floral (jasmine, lavender), complex sweetness, light-roast vibrancy. **Functional boosts:** mushroom (lion’s mane, chaga for focus/immunity), adaptogens, protein. Dessert-inspired twists (pistachio, yuzu, salted maple, ube, pandan, coconut). Single-origin traceability and unique fermentation profiles.
**Dislikes / Pain Points:** High bitterness/acidity in dark roasts for some, overly acidic or “sour” light roasts if unbalanced, generic mass-market taste, lack of convenience, unsustainable sourcing.

### **Emerging Value-Added Opportunities:**
**Light-roast single-origin packs optimized for pour-over/cold brew** — highlight Ethiopian natural brightness and floral/fruity notes.
**RTD or concentrate formats** — easy-mix Ethiopian cold brew bases with subtle fruit or functional additives.
**Functional blends** — Ethiopian beans + mushroom/adaptogen mixes for wellness (cognitive boost, lower stress).
**Dessert/global fusion** — pistachio or yuzu-infused Ethiopian lattes, or ready packs with spice notes (cardamom influence from Ethiopian tradition).
**Sustainable/traceable storytelling** — direct-trade, carbon-neutral, farmer-story packaging to justify premium pricing.
Customizable home kits — roast levels + brewing guides matching global trends (lighter for fruity profiles).
